Used & Refurbished Cameras & Electronics

Looking to save hundreds (or even thousands) of dollars on equipment to fuel your passion? Make B&H Photo’s Used Department your first stop. From gently used cameras to pre-owned pro gear, we maintain a wide selection of secondhand electronics that can save you loads of cash, whether you want to save on sound systems, smartphones, TVs or video equipment.

We know your stuff—and we understand the importance of owning gear you can rely on. That’s why every used item we sell goes through rigorous, painstaking rounds of inspection and testing to confirm it’s in good working order before being offered for sale. Each item in our used inventory also carries a 90-day parts and labor warranty.

And for you connoisseurs: B&H Photo regularly offers vintage gear, hard-to-find rarities and antique equipment, often in pristine and near-mint condition. Whether you’re looking to round out a collection or replace a cherished heirloom, feel free to give us a call.
